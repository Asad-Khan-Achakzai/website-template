About Strategy

ZATech Systems uses the following sequence and approach in all engagements with its clients:

  1. Free call to understand your needs
  2. Our proposal to meet your needs
  3. Signing of contract
  4. Refinement of steps 1 and 2
  5. Detailed high level design document created
  6. Kick off of software craft activities , with routine project management to keep activities on track
  7. Delivery
  8. User Acceptance Testing
